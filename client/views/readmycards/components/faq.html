FAQ

What does ReadMyCards.eu do?
It reads the contents of your smartcards. That's it really. If you've always wondered what information is stored on your cards, this is the tool to use.
What reader should I use?
Any smartcard reader should work with ReadMyCards.eu. For example, you can use the reader your bank provided for signing online banking transactions.
If I read a card with this service, is any of the data saved?
No, never.
So ReadMyCards.eu does not save any personal info?
Information that could potentially be used to identify a user is never saved. When you request to download Trust1Connector, we do collect some generic statistics: your approximate geolocation, the operating system and browser type and version the request was made from. Additionally, if you wish to receive news/updates from us, we will save your email address. You may remove your email address from our database at any time.
What is Trust1Connector?
Simply put, Trust1Connector is a small piece of software that allows us to communicate with smartcard readers. More detailed info can be found at Trust1Connector.com.
Is it true that Trust1Connector can be an alternative to FEDICT middleware?
Yes, it is true. Due to security issues, Java applets will no longer be supported by most browsers. Trust1Connector, as a communication layer broker, is a secure connector between your reader and your browser, serving as a FEDICT middleware alternative. For business integrations, please get in touch with us.
I am an integrator and am interested in using Trust1Connector as my middleware and integration software. What should I do?
Just get in touch with us and we will walk you through the integration process.
I have developed a container for a specific smartcard. How can I contribute to the Trust1Connector project?
We are recurrently adding more smartcard plugins/containers to Trust1Connector. Contact us so we can welcome your contribution to the project!
Is ReadMyCards.eu free to use?
Yes, absolutely. The service is completely free for private use.
