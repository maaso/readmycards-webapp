FAQ

What does ReadMyCards.eu do?
It reads the contents of your smartcards. That's it really. If you've always wondered what information is stored on your cards, this is the tool to use.
What reader should I use?
Any reader that reads smartcards should work with ReadMyCards.eu. For example, you could use the reader your bank provided to you for signing online banking transactions.
If I read a card with this service, is any of the data saved?
No, never.
So ReadMyCards.eu does not save any personal info?
Information that could potentially be used to identify a user is never saved. When you request to download Trust1Connector, we do collect some statistics: your approximate geolocation, the operating system and browser type and version the request was made from.
What is Trust1Connector?
Simply put, Trust1Connector is a small piece of software that allows us to communicate with smartcard readers. More detailed info can be found here.
Is ReadMyCards.eu free to use?
Yes, absolutely. The service is completely free for private use.
Can I use ReadMyCards and/or Trust1Connector in a business project?
For project integrations, please refer to the Trust1Connector website.
